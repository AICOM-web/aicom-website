If your internet connection is down, follow these troubleshooting steps to identify the problem and get back online:

1. Check Your Modem Lights

  • LOS (Red Blinking): This means "Loss of Signal." There is likely a break in the fiber line or a local outage. This requires technical assistance; please report it.
  • PON (Blinking or Off): The modem is trying to connect to the network. If it doesn't turn solid green after a few minutes, there is a configuration issue.

2. Basic Troubleshooting (Power Cycle)

  • Turn off the modem and unplug it from the power outlet.
  • Wait for 30 seconds.
  • Plug it back in and wait 3–5 minutes for the lights to stabilize.

3. Check Your Load (For Surf2Sawa Users)

Since S2S is prepaid, your connection will cut off instantly if your subscription expires. Message the official Facebook Page: AICOM S2S to process your load or contact our hotline numbers. Simply top up to restore the connection immediately.

4. Contact AICOM or Converge Support
